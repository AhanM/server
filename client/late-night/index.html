Late Night Hacks is an actually serious computer science project group at UCSD, part of CSES.
We meet on fridays at 8PM in the CSE Building Lobby.

What do I do there?

In the first ten minutes you decide on a goal you would like to accomplish, then you spend the next 60 minutes accomplishing it. After 60 minutes, we share what we learned.

But I don't have any goals and that sounds really awkward.

It's not, don't worry. All you need is two sentences.

Which two sentences?

One describing something that you would like to be better at, and one describing your plan to get better at it.

Chill.

I'll see you there.
